## Spiritforged Errata

Source: https://riftbound.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/news/rules-and-releases/riftbound-spiritforged-errata/

### Origins cards updated in Spiritforged patch

| # | Card | Change |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | Falling Star | Changed "Do this twice: Deal 3 to a unit" to listing effect twice separately |
| 2 | Icathian Rain | Changed "Do this 6 times: Deal 2 to a unit" to listing effect explicitly |
| 3 | Reinforce | Updated to "banish a unit from among them, then play it" |

### Spiritforged cards

| # | Card | Change |

| 4 | Arise! | Changed "ready two of them" to "ready up to two of them" |

| 7 | Edge of Night | Added "(here)" to clarify battlefield attachment location |

| 13 | Tianna Crownguard | Changed "score points" to "gain points" |
