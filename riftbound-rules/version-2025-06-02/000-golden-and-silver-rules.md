000. Golden and Silver Rules
001.  Golden Rule
002.  Card text supersedes rules text. Whenever a card fundamentally contradicts the rules, the card's
indication is what is true.
050.  Silver Rule
051.  Card text uses different terminology than rules. Card text should be interpreted according to these
rules, not as though it were text within these rules.
052.  Card , when written in card effects, is shorthand for " Main Deck  card." Runes, legends, and
battlefields are not considered cards when executing the abilities and effects of game objects. They
are considered cards for the purposes of these rules.
053.  Cards refer to themselves in the first person.
053.1.  Units and legends say "I," "me," etc.
053.2.  Gear and spells say "this."
053.3.  Battlefields say "here."
